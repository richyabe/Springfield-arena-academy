About Us

Springfields Arena Academy is committed to providing a high-quality education. Our mission is to create an environment where learning is engaging, enjoyable, and effective.

We believe that every child has unique talents and potential, and our approach focuses on nurturing these abilities while ensuring academic excellence. With experienced educators and modern facilities, we provide a holistic educational experience that prepares students for future challenges.

Our Academic Programs

Creche

Our Early Years Foundation Stage program provides a nurturing environment where young children can develop essential skills through play-based learning and structured activities.

Ages 0-3

Nursery

Building on the foundation of our creche program, our nursery offers structured learning experiences that prepare children for primary school with focus on cognitive, social, and emotional development.

Ages 3-5

Primary School

We follow a comprehensive curriculum ensuring a comprehensive education that meets both local and international standards.

Ages 5-12

Secondary School

Our secondary program continues to provide a rigorous academic foundation while fostering critical thinking, creativity, and personal development for students aged 12-18.
